About Us

Al Khatib Company was Established in 2004

Al Khatib Company is a leading company in the field of manufacturing all types of production lines and steam boilers, and equipping kitchens and hotels with all the necessary stainless steel. The company is distinguished by its extensive and specialized experience in this field, and provides innovative and high-quality solutions to its customers.

Al Khatib Company is distinguished by a professional and well-trained work team, always striving to achieve customer satisfaction and meet their needs with the highest standards of quality and efficiency. The company is also interested in developing its technologies and innovations to provide the latest technologies and innovative solutions to its customers.

Using high-quality materials and modern technologies, Al-Khatib Company is considered an ideal choice for companies and institutions looking for integrated and effective solutions with the highest level of quality and performance.
